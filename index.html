④ Outer-loop self-improvement & honest attribution④ 外层自我改进 & 诚实归因

The outer loop demonstrably self-improves when the seed has headroom: on the weak Qwen-1.5B, starting from a single-shot seed (dev_acc 33%), the haiku proposer discovered E=2·it=3·vote and the loop accepted it → 67% (+33pp), then correctly rejected the next no-gain mutation. But on the LCB-v6 dev set the seed harness was already good, and the meta-search found nothing beating it (all 4 proposals tied → all rejected, no overfitting). So the frontier lift in ② is the inner-harness effect transferring across models — not a meta-optimizer gain on that benchmark. The strict-δ rule never mistook noise for progress in any run; the decisive ablation (meta-H* ≥ hand-designed ≥ base) is built in. This honest split (when the outer loop helps vs. when the inner loop carries it) is exactly the "attribution" question the method paper must answer.当 seed 有提升空间时,外层会切实自我改进:在弱模型 Qwen-1.5B 上,从单发 seed(dev_acc 33%)出发,haiku proposer 发现了 E=2·it=3·vote 并被接受 → 67%(+33pp),随后正确拒绝了下一个无增益变异。但在 LCB v6 dev 集上 seed 本就不弱,元搜索没找到更优解(4 个提议全部持平 → 全拒,无过拟合)。所以 ② 里的前沿提升是内层 harness 跨模型迁移的效果,而非该 benchmark 上的元优化增益。严格 δ 准则在所有运行中都没把噪声当进步;决定性消融(meta-H* ≥ 手工 ≥ base)已内置。这个诚实的区分(外层何时起作用 vs 内层何时主导)正是 method paper 必须回答的「归因」问题。
